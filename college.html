Natasha Campanaro's College

During my senior year of high school, I applied to three schools and was accepted into all of them. After careful consideration, I committed to Florida Gulf Coast University.

I started at Florida Gulf Coast University in Fall of 2017 and declared my major as Mathematics. My expected graduation date is Spring of 2021.

During my first year at Florida Gulf Coast University I made Dean's List. I was elected Treasurer of my resident hall's Area Council. Through this position I work with a team to create programs and events to build the community within the resident hall. I also joined math club and was initiated into the sorority Alpha Chi Omega. I was accpeted into National Honors Society. I was also awarded the amazing oppurtunity to attend Alternative Spring Break. Alternative Spring Break is a program where you give up your spring break to go and volunteer over fourty hours of community service in an in-need community. For my trip we went to New York to do service.
