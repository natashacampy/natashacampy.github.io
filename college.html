College

During my senior year of high school, I applied to three schools and was accepted into all of them. After careful consideration, I committed to Florida Gulf Coast University. I started here in Fall of 2017 and declared my major as Mathematics. My expected graduation date is Spring of 2021.

First Year

During my first year at Florida Gulf Coast University I had many accomplishments listed below.

  • Made Dean's List.
  • Accepted into Honors College.
  • Was elected Treasurer of my resident hall's Area Council. Through this position I work with a team to create programs and events to build the community within the resident hall. At the end of the year, my team won Area Council of the 2017-2018 school year.
  • Became an active member of Math Club.
  • Was initiated into the Kappa Chi chapter of Alpha Chi Omega sorority. I was also elected Freshman Representative of Standards Board.
  • Was initiated into the Florida Gulf Coast University chapter of Phi Eta Sigma National Honor Society.
  • Accepted into the Alternative Break Program. Alternative Break is a program where during your spring break you go and volunteer over forty hours of community service in an in-need community. For my trip we went to New York to do service.

The courses I took my first year of college are listed below.

  • Pre-Calculus
  • Introduction to Literature
  • Introduction to Sociology
  • Introduction to Psychology
  • Statistical Methods
  • Calculus One
  • Introduction to Entrepreneurship
  • Introduction to Computer Science
  • American Literature
  • Human Systems

Second Year

At the end of my first year, I was hired into two positions to begin my second year at Florida Gulf Coast University. Those positions are

  • Resident Assistant for the Live and Learn Community for Women in Science, Technology, Engineering, Mathematics, and Health Science (WiStem).
  • Florida Gulf Coast University Student Ambassador.

I began my second year holding the following positions:

  • Vice President of Florida Gulf Coast University's Math Club.
  • Freshman chair of the Standards Board of Kappa Chi chapter of Alpha Chi Omega sorority.
