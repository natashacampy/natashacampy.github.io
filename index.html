Introduction

       

Hello! My name is Natasha Campanaro and I am a student at Florida Gulf Coast University. I am from a beach community in west- central Florida. I love learning new things, being outside in the subshine, and spending time with family and friends.

I was born and raised in Palm Harbor, Florida. Palm Harbor is a town in west-central Florida, near Clearwater and Tampa. I am an only child and am very close with my parents as well as my extended family. In Kindergarten, I started Tae Kwon Do at Dunedin Tae Kwon Do Academy in Dunedin, Florida.
