Creating a Website

I made this website in the spring semester of 2018 in Introduction to Computer Science. I used and refrences websites and programs such as GitHub, Brackets, and w3schools.

When creating my website, I took precautions to make sure it was usable to all. I will describe my precautions from the top of the page down. Each image has alternative text for those who require text-only. The navigation menu uses only short key-words to direct users easily and clearly. It also carries a consistent order and is at the top of each page. Each page uses the same text font and size to carry a neat and professional format. I hope the precautions I took are useful to all.
